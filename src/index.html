About

Software engineering is the intersection of two of my passions: computer technology and problem-solving. I'm a self-starter, motivated to craft beautiful software that fulfills business objectives in a way that's enjoyable for both businesses and end-users. I love a challenge and am constantly learning. I have experience across many languages, platforms, and frameworks -- I'm never intimidated to tackle a new one. I believe strongly in a few core concepts:

  • Business objectives/requirements should dictate the technology stack rather than making a particular tech stack try to fit all projects
  • Software should be written with the long-term vision and goals in mind to aid in decisions about architecture, tests, documentation, and maintainability
  • Complex problems should be broken down into smaller problems that can be more easily reasoned about and transformed into code that is more understandable and maintainable
  • Properly designed systems and architectures create momentum as new products and features are created
